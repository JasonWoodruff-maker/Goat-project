The Goat aka my Mom

My Mom is my goat for millions of reasons; One of those reasons is that she always believed in me even when I thought no one did. My Mom was always very supportive of everything I did especially sports and school, she would never let me give up on my dream which is why I have made it so far in school and everything else in my life. Even if I haven't been the best in a ton of ways, she looks right past that and makes me feel like everything is safe and the reason people make mistakes is for them and others to learn from them.
